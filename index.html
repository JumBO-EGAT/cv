Pipat Chitnumsab (JumBO)

Engineer level 11 of the Electricity Generating Authority of Thailand (EGAT).

I started working at EGAT from September, 1996 in System Planning division. I have experiences on power system analysis and long-term planning including applying power system analysis softwares e.g. PSS/E from Siemens PTI, PowerFactory from DIgSILENT, and so on. I am interested in grid stability analysis, grid integration for renewable energy resources and new technologies, grid connection for regional power trading. In addtion, the new trend in self-consumption and micro grid technology is in my challenge in the main grid operation. I am also interested in data analytics about the changing behavior of customers. I will prepare myself and my organization to support the variation in renewable generation, ambiguity of customer needs, affecting uncertainty of load demand growth and increasing the complexity of operation and planning.
